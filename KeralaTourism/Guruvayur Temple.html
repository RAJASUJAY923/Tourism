Spiritual Draws & Customs at Guruvayur Temple

Guruvayur Temple is not just a place of worship; it's a hub of spiritual activity. Some key draws and customs include:

  • Daily Rituals: The temple has a full schedule of daily rituals, including morning and evening prayers, that attract devotees year-round.
  • Festivals: Major festivals like Guruvayur Ekadasi, Janmashtami, and Vishu are celebrated with great enthusiasm, drawing large crowds.
  • Elephant Processions: The temple's elephants play a significant role in ceremonies and festivals, adding to the vibrant atmosphere.
  • Offerings: Devotees often bring offerings such as flowers, coconuts, and sweets to seek blessings from Lord Guruvayurappan.
  • Prasadam: The temple is famous for its prasadam (sacred food), especially the 'Nivedyam' made of rice and jaggery, which is distributed to devotees.
